About

gfxpoly is a library for doing geometric polygon operations. (like polygon intersection, union, hidden surface removal, stroke to outline conversion, wind rule conversion etc.)
It's distributed under a non-commercial license (BSD-2).

It's very stable- e.g. at www.scribd.com, it's used to process (intersect) over 150,000,000 polygons (stemming from figures and illustrations in document uploads) every single day.
